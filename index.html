Frequently Asked Questions

Start by filling out our online inquiry form or emailing us your background details. Our case managers will then schedule an initial assessment to design your personalized relocation plan.

We arrange relocations to over 20 partner countries across North America, Europe, and Oceania. Exact destinations depend on eligibility criteria, family preferences, and local sponsorship availability.

We handle visa applications, travel bookings, medical screenings, and arrival logistics. You’ll have a dedicated case manager guiding you from departure through local registration in your new country.

Timelines vary by country and individual circumstances but typically range from 3 to 6 months from application to arrival. We’ll provide an estimated schedule during your assessment.

No, there are no costs for refugees. HopeBridge is fully funded by our generous sponsors and partners. All essential relocation expenses—including visas, flights, and initial housing—are completely covered by individual sponsors .

After arrival, we provide cultural orientation, language classes, job referral assistance, and ongoing mentorship to help you integrate smoothly into your new community.
